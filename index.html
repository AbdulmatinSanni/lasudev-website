About Us

Our Goals and Objectives

LasuDevs is a community created by some group of students in LASU to create an ecosystem that will both help, inspire, learn, support and possibly improve young talented programmers at Lagos State University.

This initiative is as a result of the current trend of the world, we believe IT is needed in almost all field in the world and the application of knowledge of IT into your field of studies would make it 'waw'.
